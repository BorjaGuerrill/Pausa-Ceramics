Pause and read,

The earth and colors make magic when it comes to ceramic pottery. Each piece embraces thoughts, illusions and moments of peace. What we sometimes want in appearance, get transformed in a new creation, but not for that less appealing. Our ever agitated existence comes to a pause through the times of ceramics. The no-rush time of molding, firing, glazing and drying, remind us that every thing has its own path and its own rhythm.
